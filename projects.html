Extracting Interests from Student's Story

This Kaggle notebook explores a natural language processing (NLP) approach to extracting students' interests from their personal stories. By leveraging text analysis techniques such as tokenization, keyword extraction, and sentiment analysis, the model identifies patterns and key themes in students' narratives to determine their areas of interest. Implementation involves preprocessing the text data, applying NLP libraries like NLTK or spaCy, and using machine learning or rule-based methods to categorize interests. This can be applied in educational settings to personalize learning experiences, career counseling, and student engagement initiatives.

Robotics

Zumo Bot

This Zumo bot is a color-responsive line-following robot that adapts its movement based on surface reflections. Equipped with color sensors, it follows a designated path while dynamically adjusting its speed. When it detects black, it accelerates, and when it sees white, it decelerates, continuously responding to color changes in real-time. This behavior allows the bot to navigate efficiently while demonstrating interactive and adaptive motion control, making it ideal for robotics experiments and automation projects.

Documentary

From Kabul to Bard

This documentary provides a deep look into the journeys of Afghan students who fled Afghanistan following its collapse in 2021 and found a new home at Bard College. It captures their struggles, resilience, and adaptation to a new life, highlighting their experiences during the crisis, the challenges of displacement, and their pursuit of education in a foreign land. Through personal stories, the film sheds light on their past, their hopes for the future, and how they are building new opportunities while staying connected to their roots.

Banoo Handmade

"Banoo Handmade" is a short documentary that tells the inspiring story of an Afghan woman who, while pursuing her studies in architecture, launched a handmade jewelry business. Her mission extends beyond personal success; she aims to empower Afghan women by creating job opportunities and sustainable income streams, fostering economic independence within her community.
